Coaching

Next to our online community, we also offer individual coaching for those who prefer to go a step further into their process of exploring and self-finding. Like the community, our coaching's main focus is on you and your needs. This is whay you are the one who decides in which direction you want to go and what you want to achieve. All our coaches have a professional background and a patient ear and are happy to help you in every life situation to choose the right path for you.

Services

The services that summit provides can vary depending on the commodity and availability of the client. As a starter, we offer a large supportive online community where people can join and share their experiences. Everybody has the chance to participate and be involved in giving their comments and receiving them as well. We also hope to get a more extensive community in the future. Additionally, the client can get a more personalized service in which they can get direct help with a professional coach face-to-face; thus, it is possible to monitor and see the client's development more thoroughly.
