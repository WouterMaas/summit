Welcome!

Next to our online community, we also offer individual coaching for those who prefer to go a step further into their process of exploring and self-finding. Like the community, our coaching's main focus is on you and your needs. This is whay you are the one who decides in which direction you want to go and what you want to achieve. All our coaches have a professional background and a patient ear and are happy to help you in every life situation to choose the right path for you.
These are our Rules:
  • Our community is a strong supporter of tolerance and freedom of speech and expression. Everybody is allowed to share whatever they want to share except it is intolerant, harmful or inappropriate for this platform.
  • Every person in this community is consciously deciding to share something private information about themselves, their lives and their experiences. To be able to keep our cummunity a safe and open space, nobody should get negatively judged on what they share or pressured to share more than they want.
  • We as administration are usually not involving ourselves in the community and the flow of communication within it, despite we see that there is any kind of harm done.
